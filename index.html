Ingredients

Click here for Ingredient images

  1. 2 Noodle Nests
  2. 1 Carrot
  3. 1/3 Savoy Cabbage
  4. 5 Broccolini Florets
  5. 2 tbsp Schewan Sauce
  6. 2 tbsp Soya Sauce
  7. 1/2 tsp Black Pepper
  8. 1/2 tsp Paprika
  9. 1/4 tsp Parsley
  10. 1 tbsp Oil
  11. 3 cloves of garlic

Garlic Schewan Noodles

Serves 2 portions

Step 1

Start to heat up your water to a low boil.

Step 2

Whilst your water is heating, cut up your vegetables into small strips and dice up 3 cloves of garlic.

Step 3

Start heating up your pan on a low-medium heat and pour in the oil and pour in the diced garlic.

Step 4

As the garlic starts sizzling, place all your vegetables into the pan to start frying.

Step 5

Pour the pre-measured ingredients including the soya sauce, Schewan sauce, and your spices into a seperate bowl to create your sauce mix

Whilst doing so, place your noodles to cook into your now boiling water and cook as per the packet instructions.

Step 6

Once your vegetables seem par fried, drain your noodles leaving about 8tsps of water, and mix your noodles into the pan with the vegetables.

Turning the heat up to medium-high, pour in your sauce mix and saute all ingredients together until everything is combined.

Plate up and ENJOY!
